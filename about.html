About Us

We are joy to discover our about us

Aleef Travel Services (P) Ltd., Ltd., a Travel Management Company, started its operation in Sep 1998 and grew as a leading Travel Management Company in Chennai. Having extensive goodwill with the clients and high credibility in the industry circles, it has now established a brand name in the Travel Industry.

The Company is IATA accredited. It is also recognized by Ministry of Tourism and legitimately holds the active membership of TAAI & TAFI.

This venture is backed by qualified promoter – Mr. Sadiq Ali, He have 2 decades of varied travel industry experience. Our promoter is efficiently backed by a strong team of Industry professionals manning different activities competently & consistently.

We render value added services by delivering effective & cost sensitive Travel Solutions to our clients. We are committed to providing high quality services by maintaining focus on the changing needs of our itinerant. We measure our performance in delivering the services and set standards for ourselves. We efficiently blend traditional hospitality, which is the hallmark of this industry with a dynamic professional approach backed by the state of Art Technology.

We regularly audit routes & fares on behalf of our customers and recommend best buy initiatives that balance the correct degree of flexibility with the least cancellation and amendment fees. It has helped us to attain respectable turnover and now the company boasts of key corporate accounts in its Portfolio.
